Welcome to Super Simple English

English is the third most spoken language worldwide. Between 600-700 million of people speak English as a foreign language.

Would you like to be one of them ?

On a daily basis we often find situations where knowing English could be of a great help; at work, while on holidays, with online search engines or even helping your kids with their homework. Come and see us, whatever your interest!

Our objective at Super Simple English is that you can make yourself understood and you understand others.

Come and enjoy your lessons. We want you to learn having fun, using games and role plays.


The little ones will have a great time singing, playing games and with different activities that after several repetitions will allow them to learn English the same way they learn the mother tongue.
